Our Beginnings

We began our tea journey back in 2015 starting with an idea to make good tea. Our founder, Tommie Rogers, wanted to make a place where it didn't feel like a corporate store, but like a study at home. A place where you can relax, sip your favorite tea, and either focus on a good book or a good discussion. With his experience in the food industry, he knows how to supply the shop with tasty treats, but not the tea. With help from the community, he was able to make some connections to help with the tea side of things. Thankful for the generosity of the community, he gave the tea shop its signature name in reminder of the community that helped make this dream possible. So please, sit back, relax, and enjoy the simplicity, the quality, and the Jenerossi Tea.
